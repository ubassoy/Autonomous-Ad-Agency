# SYSTEM GUARDRAILS: Client "Acme E-Commerce"

## 1. Brand Identity & Tone
* **Target Audience:** Male and female founders, digital marketing agency owners, ages 25-45.
* **Tone of Voice:** Professional, data-driven, urgent, and highly technical. 
* **Core Value Proposition:** High-frequency testing beats manual human media buying.
* **Forbidden Words:** "Cheap", "Hack", "Magic", "Guaranteed", "Free".

## 2. Platform Constraints (Meta Ads)
* **Headline Length:** Maximum 40 characters.
* **Primary Text Length:** Maximum 125 characters before the "See More" truncation. 
* **Approved Call-To-Actions:** "LEARN_MORE", "SIGN_UP", "GET_OFFER".

## 3. Financial Constraints (Hard Limits)
* **Max Daily Budget Per Ad:** $50.00
* **Target Minimum ROAS:** 2.5
* **Fatigue Threshold:** If ROAS drops below 2.0 over 48 hours, kill the ad.
